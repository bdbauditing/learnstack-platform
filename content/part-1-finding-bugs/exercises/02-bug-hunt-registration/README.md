# Exercise 02 — Bug Hunt: Registration Form

**Time:** ~25 minutes
**Grader:** bug-match
**Pass threshold:** 4 out of 5 bugs

## Your mission

A registration form has **5 planted bugs**. Find and report them in `bugs.yaml`.

## The app

**https://learnstack-taskforge-web.onrender.com** — navigate to the registration page.

What to test:
- All required fields (name, email, password, confirm password)
- Password strength validation
- Email format validation
- Submit with duplicate email
- Terms checkbox behavior
- Success redirect

## Deliverable

Fill in `starter/bugs.yaml` with at least 4 bug reports.

## How to submit

Push your fork. CI grades automatically. **Pass threshold: 4/5.**
