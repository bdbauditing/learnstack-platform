# [Billing] Total price does not update when seat count changes

**Severity:** High
**Priority:** High

## Environment

- **Browser:** Firefox 130
- **OS:** Windows 11
- **Viewport:** 1920x1080
- **URL:** https://learnstack-taskforge-web.onrender.com/billing

## Steps to Reproduce

1. Log in with `bob@taskforge.io` / `Password1!` (press Enter — submit button is a known bug)
2. Navigate to `/billing`
3. Select any subscription plan
4. Click the **+** button to increase seat count from 1 to 3
5. Observe the total price displayed

## Expected Behavior

The total price updates immediately to reflect the new seat count (unit price × seats).

## Actual Behavior

The total price remains unchanged at the original 1-seat price after changing the seat count.
